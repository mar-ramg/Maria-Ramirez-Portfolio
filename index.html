About Me

Ever since I was 4 years old I was fascinated by perfecting my drawing skills about horses. I remember vividly experiencing motivation, determination, fearlessness, and inspiration from artists to create my own. Throughout my 20 years of living, I have explored painting, ceramics, drawing, illustration, and digital art. I'm mainly involved in graphic design related from making logos to creating websites.

I'm enrolled at San Joaquin Delta College to receive my Associate's Degree in Graphic Design and enrolled at the University of Texas at Austin to receive my Web Development certificate. I love to learn anything that has to do with art because there is something new because of how art is evolving.

I'm an upcoming graphic designer with a lot of passion for art. As a designer, I strive to offer and help any client's dream with their brand in logos, business cards, websites, etc. I like to communicate with my client's projects for the result to come out with the best design solution. I'll let my portfolio speak for itself. Have fun clicking and scrolling!
